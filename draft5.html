Geovisualization of obesity and type-2 diabetes by states in the USA

Obesity and Type-2 Diabetes in the USA

The map beside gives the option to zoom in the map. The burger menu on the top-right corner of the map provides the option to select different base layers and choropleth map or heatmap. The choropleth map represents the obesity rates by state, where darker shades indicate higher obesity rates. Hovering the mouse on the states will display both the obesity and diabetes rate for a particular state. Additionally, the heatmap layer showcases the prevalence of type-2 diabetes across the country.

Obesity and type-2 diabetes are significant health issues in the United States. It is essential to address these health concerns through education, awareness, and promoting a healthy lifestyle that includes regular physical activity and balanced nutrition.
